Dumpling

how to make dumpling

Dumpling is a broad class of dishes that consist of pieces of dough wrapped around a filling, or of dough with no filling.

Ingredients

  • flour
  • meat
  • cabbage
  • egg
  1. make the dough with flour and water and knead
  2. separate dough into circles
  3. use a food processor to grind cabbage and use the processed pork and mix with cabbage and egg with a bit of salt
  4. wrap the filling in dough and place in boiling water
