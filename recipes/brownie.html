Brownie

how to make brownie

A chocolate brownie or simply a brownie is a square or rectangular chocolate baked confection. Brownies come in a variety of forms and may be either fudgy or cakey, depending on their density.

Ingredients

  • flour
  • cocoa powder
  • eggs
  • chocolate chips
  • milk
  1. mix all dry ingredients and mix all wet ingredients
  2. fold ingredients in together
  3. put on a baking pan and bake it
